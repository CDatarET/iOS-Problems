Managing Multiple Responsibilities Without Feeling Overwhelmed
Problem

Many people struggle to balance work, personal life, errands, appointments, and self-care. Existing calendar and to-do list apps help organize tasks, but they often don't help users prioritize realistically or adjust plans when unexpected events occur.

Many users end up using:

Google Calendar for events
Notes app for reminders
WhatsApp messages to themselves
Sticky notes
Alarm apps

instead of one cohesive system.
